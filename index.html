My Favorite Holiday Destination

Club Mykonos Langebaan

5 reasons why Club Mykonos is the best holiday destination

  • You feel like you are in Greece without needing to go overseas.
  • There is many activities to keep your time occupied.
  • You can get really beautiful pictures there.
  • Theres activities and areas for any age group.
  • My favorite feature is the choices between swimming in the ocean, an outdoor pool or a heated indoor pool.
